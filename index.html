The Duality: Markets & Machines

Most people see Economics and Software Engineering as two different worlds. I see them as the same thing: **systems of logic.** My journey began at Caucasus University, where I spent my days analyzing market fluctuations and my nights deconstructing C# backend architectures. I realized that modern economics is no longer just about theory—it’s about the speed of the data pipelines and the security of the transactions.

This realization pushed me to pivot from a traditional financial path into **Full-Stack Development**, focusing on high-performance banking ecosystems.

The Methodology

In my current role at **Tera-Bank**, I don't just "write code." I engineer solutions that must be mission-critical, secure, and scalable. My approach is rooted in **meticulous data integrity**. Whether it's managing MySQL databases or implementing JWT authentication, my goal is to ensure that the backend is invisible yet invincible.

The Vision

I am building a career at the intersection of **Fintech and Data Engineering**. I believe the future of Georgia’s economy will be written in code. My mission is to be at the forefront of that transformation, building the infrastructure that allows millions of secure, real-time transactions to happen every second.
